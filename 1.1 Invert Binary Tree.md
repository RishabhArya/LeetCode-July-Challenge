<h1 id="invert-binary-tree">Invert Binary Tree</h1>
<p>Invert a binary tree.</p>
<p><strong>Example:</strong></p>
<p>Input:</p>
<pre><code>     4
   /   \
  2     7
 / \   / \
1   3 6   9
</code></pre>
<p>Output:</p>
<pre><code>     4
   /   \
  7     2
 / \   / \
9   6 3   1
</code></pre>
